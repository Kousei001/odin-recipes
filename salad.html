Description

A light and healthy salad filled with crisp vegetables and a zesty dresskng.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Carrots
  • Salad dressing

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Mix them in a large bowl.
  3. Drizzle with dressing and toss well.
